Description

Comprehensive introduction to computer vision: image processing, detection & recognition, geometry- and physics-based vision, and video analysis. Emphasis on concepts and hands-on solutions to real-world vision problems.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand principles of image formation & analysis in 2D/3D vision.
  • Use core methods for registration, alignment, matching.
  • Explore categorization & segmentation.
  • Apply techniques to build practical CV applications.
